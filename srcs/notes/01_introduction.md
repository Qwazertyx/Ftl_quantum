-in quantum computeers, qubits are entangled, it allows the computer to manipulate 2^N states simultaneously using just Nqubits


quantum measurement
-active process that interacts with the qubit, forces it out of its superposition and makes it choose a definite state (1 or 0)
-how it works (amplifies one of the qubit potential state (what we see as the result)) (vending machine analogy is nice -> can be everything until the measurment (the choice) until it can only be one soda, cant be a juice or water anymore)
-quantum algorithms are designed to manipulated probabilities so that when we finally measure, the state collapses to the correct answer with high probability
-need to run the computation many times to build a statistical picture (500 times with 250/250 means 50%)
